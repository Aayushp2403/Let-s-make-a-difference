Stories from the Field

Every day, we witness the power of community support and compassion. These photos capture moments of hope, transformation, and the incredible impact we're making together in our community.

Pizza Donation from Dominos

Thanks to the generous donation from Dominos, we were able to bring fresh, hot pizzas to the Grace Center of Hope. This photo shows us preparing to deliver these meals to families and individuals who needed them most.

Community partnerships like this with local businesses make such a meaningful difference. Every pizza delivered represents not just a meal, but a moment of joy and the knowledge that the community cares about those facing difficult times.

Costco Gift Card Donations

Thanks to Costco's generous donation of gift cards, we were able to provide essential resources to both Grace Center of Hope and Turning Point. These gift cards allow these organizations to purchase exactly what their communities need most - from food and personal care items to household essentials.

Corporate partnerships like this amplify our impact tremendously. By working together with businesses like Costco and organizations like Grace Center of Hope and Turning Point, we can reach more people and provide more comprehensive support to those who need it most.
